Frequently Asked Questions

What are the team sizes?

1-4 members. If more than 5 people attend without first contacting an executive, the project will be dismissed and not be judged.

What's the Theme?

The themes will always be somewhat medically related, if you create a project utilizing AI or any other computer software, and link it to the theme, which will be given on the day of, then you'll do very well for your project!

What is a MediHacks?

To begin, we first need to answer the question of, what is a hackathon? A hackathon is a coding competition where programmers are able to use their skills and knowledge to complete a project. In a MedHackathon, we incorporate the same aspects you would expect from a hackathon, but we focus primarily on medicine. Our MedHackathons tackle medical issues and bring practical solutions to real life problems. For example, submissions could include a basic disease identifier, notifier app, and ingenuitive sleep trackers!

What will there be at Hackathons?

At a hackathon, we will have multiple guest speakers, workshops, and other noteworthy events that will teach you about coding and give you tips and tricks for hackathons!
